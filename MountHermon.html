The Majestic Mount Hermon

Mount Hermon is the highest mountain in Israel. It is known for its snowy peak during winter and its beautiful natural scenery throughout the year.

Experience the Snowy Slopes of Mount Hermon

Mount Hermon is a popular destination for winter sports such as skiing and snowboarding. During warmer seasons, visitors can enjoy hiking trails, mountain views, and the refreshing climate of this famous mountain.

4 Facts About Mount Hermon

1. Mount Hermon is the highest mountain in Israel, reaching over 2,800 meters above sea level.

2. It is the only place in Israel where visitors can ski during winter.

3. The mountain lies near the borders of Syria and Lebanon.

4. Mount Hermon is an important source of water for rivers that flow into the Sea of Galilee.
