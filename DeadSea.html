The Natural Wonder of the Dead Sea

The Dead Sea is one of the most famous natural attractions in Israel. Known as the lowest point on Earth, this unique lake contains extremely salty water that allows people to float easily on its surface. Many tourists visit the area to relax, take photos, and experience this natural phenomenon.

Experience the Unique Waters of the Dead Sea

The Dead Sea is also known for its mineral-rich mud and therapeutic properties. Visitors from around the world come to enjoy spa treatments, relax in resorts, and experience the peaceful desert landscape surrounding the lake.

4 Facts About the Dead Sea

1. The Dead Sea lies about 430 meters below sea level.

2. It is nearly 10 times saltier than regular ocean water.

3. Very few plants and animals can survive in its water due to the high salt level.

4. It borders both Israel and Jordan.
